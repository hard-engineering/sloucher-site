sshhh

Push-to-talk dictation in your menu bar.

Hold the Option key and speak. Release, and sshhh transcribes your words on your Mac and pastes them into whatever app you are using - editor, browser, chat, mail, or terminal. No audio leaves your device.

Menu-bar only On-device transcription No account, no cloud macOS 14+ · Apple Silicon

How it works

  1. Grant permissions when prompted on first launch - Microphone to record speech, and Accessibility to paste transcribed text into other apps.
  2. Hold Option and speak. A small floating widget shows when sshhh is recording.
  3. Release Option. sshhh transcribes the audio on your Mac and pastes the text into the active app.
  4. Add custom vocabulary in the Dictionary for names, jargon, and technical terms. Entries can also expand a short spoken form into a longer phrase.

Common tips: if transcribed text is not inserted, check that sshhh is enabled under System Settings › Privacy & Security › Accessibility. If recording does not start, check Microphone access under Privacy & Security › Microphone. sshhh requires macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later on an Apple Silicon Mac.

Privacy

sshhh transcribes audio locally on your Mac and does not upload, collect, sell, or track your audio or transcripts. Read the full privacy policy.
