Sloucher

A quiet posture coach in your menu bar.

Calibrate once while sitting upright. Sloucher then watches for posture changes from your own baseline and nudges you when you start to slump - all on your Mac, nothing uploaded.

Menu-bar only On-device Vision processing No cloud account macOS 13+ · Apple Silicon

How it works

  1. Enable the camera when the setup window appears on first launch.
  2. Calibrate - sit upright with your head and shoulders visible, then click Calibrate to set your baseline.
  3. Work as normal. Sloucher stays quiet until your posture drops below your threshold, then nudges you with an optional notification, sound, and screen glow.
  4. Stay in control with Pause, Snooze, sensitivity, and a Test nudge button.
